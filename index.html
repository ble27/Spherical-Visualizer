About This Tool

This free interactive spherical region visualizer helps Calculus 3 (Multivariable Calculus) students understand 3D regions bounded by spherical coordinates. Visualize spherical regions by adjusting rho (ρ), phi (φ), and theta (θ) parameters in real-time. Perfect for understanding triple integrals and regions of integration in spherical coordinates.

How to Use

Enter your boundary values for ρ (radius), φ (phi angle from the z-axis), and θ (theta angle in the xy-plane). Type "pi" to automatically convert to π notation. Click "Update Visualization" to render the 3D region. Use your mouse to rotate, zoom, and explore the spherical region from any angle.
